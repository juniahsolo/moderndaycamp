About Us

Modern Day Campfire Collective is a vibrant, event-based initiative born from the Modern Day Campfire YouTube podcast — a platform that advocates for mental health awareness through visual arts and authentic conversations. We believe in creating safe, expressive, and healing spaces that bring people together around shared stories, creativity, and personal growth.

Our Vision

To foster a culture where mental wellness is prioritized, self-expression is celebrated, and communities are empowered through connection, creativity, and intentional experiences.

Our Services

We offer dynamic and customized sessions tailored to individuals, groups, and organizations, including:

  • Painting & Creative Expression Sessions: Guided art sessions that encourage mindfulness and emotional release.
  • Team Building Workshops: Interactive and reflective group activities designed to foster collaboration, empathy, and morale.
  • Movie Nights & Mental Health Talks: Screenings and open conversations around relatable mental health themes.
  • Bonfire Gatherings: Intimate, safe spaces for storytelling, reflection, and connection.
  • Travel & Retreat Experiences: Getaways focused on personal reflection, bonding, and experiential growth.
